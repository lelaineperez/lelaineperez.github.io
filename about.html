Lelaine Perez

Creative Digital Project Manager

Supporting games, toys, and storytelling through structure, collaboration, and adaptability.

I’m a Digital Project Manager with a strong interest in creative production spaces, particularly games, toys, and film. I’m drawn to roles where I can support creative teams by bringing clarity, organization, and momentum to projects without disrupting the creative process.

With a technical background, I’m comfortable collaborating across disciplines and adapting quickly to new tools and workflows. I enjoy wearing many hats—whether that means coordinating timelines, managing documentation, facilitating communication, or stepping in wherever a project needs support.

I’m currently focused on building hands-on experience managing collaborative projects, refining workflows, and learning how creative teams operate at different stages of production. My goal is to grow as a project manager within creative industries where technology, storytelling, and teamwork come together to create meaningful experiences.

How I Work as a Project Manager

  • Translating creative ideas into actionable plans
  • Keeping timelines, tasks, and deliverables organized
  • Supporting clear communication across creative and technical teams
  • Adapting processes to fit the project and team needs
  • Learning new tools and workflows quickly in fast-moving environments

My Project Management Style

I see project management as a support role first. My approach focuses on creating structure that empowers creative teams rather than limiting them. I value flexibility, communication, and collaboration, and I’m most effective when I can help teams stay aligned while adapting to changing project needs.

What I’m Currently Building Toward

  • Production pipelines in games and creative media
  • Cross-functional collaboration in creative teams
  • Tools and processes that support creative production
